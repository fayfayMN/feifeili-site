💻 What I Build

Tools that solve real problems — the kind I've encountered firsthand.

🩺 Team Doctor

A diagnostic tool that turns team friction into a clear action plan — combining a deterministic scoring engine with AI-generated insights. Designed for teams that know something is off but can't name it.

🤝 TeamUp

A platform for forming project teams when structure is missing. Born from real frustration with how hard it is to find the right people for the right work.

⚙️ TeamOS

Lightweight operating systems for teams — not full enterprise software, not just a Google Doc. The middle layer where most teams actually live.

💼 ZeroCode Career Coach

An AI-powered career coaching tool — helping job seekers navigate their search with structured guidance and practical feedback.

❤️ Who I Build For

I build for small businesses, nonprofits, student clubs, and early-stage teams — the organizations that need clarity but can't afford a consulting firm or a 6-month implementation.

I build for the people who feel overwhelmed by enterprise tools and abandoned by lightweight ones. The middle layer is where I live.

My tools are pointed at human problems, not just technical ones. I build for the team that needs a decision log but doesn't know what one is yet. For the founder who's drowning in ambiguity. For the club officer who inherited chaos and wants to hand over clarity.

Most software is built for enterprises with compliance budgets or consumers with attention spans. I build for the people in between — the ones who need structure and can't afford to wait.

Built by hand. No templates, no shortcuts.
